Classic Mojito

Classic Mojito: Ingredients: Fresh mint leaves, lime wedges, white rum, simple syrup, club soda, ice. Instructions: Muddle mint leaves and lime wedges with simple syrup in a glass. Fill the glass with ice, add rum, and top with club soda. Stir gently and garnish with a mint sprig.

BANGLADESHI MANGO LASSI

Ingredients: Fresh mango, yogurt, milk, sugar (optional), cardamom powder (optional), ice cubes. Instructions: Blend mango, yogurt, milk, sugar (to taste), and cardamom powder until smooth. Add ice cubes and blend again until frothy. Serve chilled..
